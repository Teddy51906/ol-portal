The Intentional Leadership Accelerator

Our flagship cohort-based program, designed for the leaders your future depends on.

In the age of AI, a leader’s competitive edge is the ability to lead with self-awareness, connection, and intention. The Intentional Leadership Accelerator is interactive, cohort-based group coaching and training where leaders don’t just learn. They evolve together. Through customized use cases, peer coaching, and built-in accountability, participants leave every session with strategies they apply the next day.

Build the program your leaders need

Choose the modules most relevant to your organization:

  • Intentional Leadership Understand the ripple effect of your behaviors and create the tone, trust, and conditions teams need to thrive.
  • Communications Bootcamp Apply the 3Cs Communication Framework and insights from a communication and thinking assessment (NBI) to improve influence and collaboration.
  • Unlock Change Navigate the psychology of resistance and lead teams through ambiguity with clarity and confidence.
  • Feedback Without Fear Deliver feedback that builds trust, creates clarity, and moves performance forward.
  • Own the Room Lead high-stakes conversations with a stakeholder-centered preparation model.
  • Coaching Bootcamp Coach for performance and growth through powerful questions and the GROWTH model.
  • Own Your Day Focus time and energy on what matters most through intentional prioritization, boundaries, and smarter delegation.

Why it works

  • Interactive group coaching. Real emotions, real challenges, real conversation, not passive instruction.
  • Real-world application. Leaders bring live use cases and leave with tools they apply immediately.
  • Built-in accountability. Every participant gets a peer coach and a check-in between sessions, so commitments get practiced, not shelved.
  • Lasting impact. Shared language and stronger connection create a ripple effect that endures after the program ends.

Participants build the capabilities that separate transactional managers from transformational leaders: self-awareness of their impact on others, strategies to manage stress and activate their best self, communication that flexes to context and audience, sharper feedback and coaching skills, and the confidence to influence without authority.

Rolling out Intentional Leadership across our leaders has been a game changer.
