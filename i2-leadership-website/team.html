Meet the Team

Laura Schwarz

Founder & Executive Coach

Laura Schwarz is the Founder of i2 Leadership, a coaching and leadership development firm helping leaders navigate change, build high-performing teams, and lead intentionally. Before launching i2 Leadership in 2009, Laura held leadership roles in Product and Global Marketing at American Express and Young & Rubicam Advertising, providing her valuable insight into leadership behaviors that drive results and the dynamics that create friction. She helps leaders refine their approach, overcome roadblocks, and make lasting shifts in how they lead.

Laura’s coaching and leadership training blends structure with adaptability, using proven frameworks, neuroscience-based practices, and real-world experience to deliver practical, relevant, and immediately applicable solutions. She tailors every engagement to her clients’ needs, reading the room and pivoting as necessary to drive results. Laura partners with a network of trusted coaches and learning professionals, allowing her to scale programs, bring in specialized expertise, and design solutions that fit each client while maintaining a personalized experience.

A graduate of the University of Michigan, Laura is certified as a Leadership Coach (CPCC) and Team Coach (EMCC Global) and is a member of The Institute of Coaching at Harvard University, TroopHR, and NYC Coaching Collective.
